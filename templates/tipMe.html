ABOUT TIP ME

OUR AIM

Creatives are bright minded individuals that bring ideas to life and these creative ideas sparks light into the life of the fans. Not more than often, people want to show appreciation to these creatives for the tremendous effort they put into their creativity. At Tip Me, we are focused on helping creatives receive gifts and money from their fans who wish to give voluntarily and simultaneously we reduce the communication barrier between creatives and their fans. With Tip Me, you can receive gifts and monetary support from your fans straight into your bank account with zero commission on all transactions. Apart from helping creatives earn monetary support from their fans, Tip Me helps creatives to be more confident in their creativity, own their creativity and take full control of how they choose to earn with their creative ideas.
